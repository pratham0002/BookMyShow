Spider-Man: Across the Verse – A Journey Through the Multiverse

The Spider-Man universe has evolved immensely since its inception, diving deeper into the concept of the multiverse with Spider-Man: Across the Verse. This journey brings various Spider-Men and Spider-Women together across different dimensions, creating a vivid mosaic of heroes, villains, and worlds. The concept of the multiverse not only expands storytelling but also opens up endless creative possibilities.

Variants

Explore the different Spider variants appearing across the multiverse:

  • Peter Parker (Classic Spider-Man): The original Spider-Man known for his iconic red and blue suit, battling crime in New York City while juggling a normal life.
  • Spider-Gwen: An alternate universe version of Gwen Stacy who becomes Spider-Woman, featuring a distinct white, black, and pink suit.
  • Spider-Man 2099: Miguel O'Hara, a futuristic Spider-Man from the year 2099, who uses advanced technology and genetic enhancements to fight crime.
  • Spider-Man Noir: A darker, grittier version of Spider-Man set in the 1930s, with a trench coat and goggles to match his noir style.

Villains

Meet some of the most formidable villains from different dimensions:

  • Green Goblin: One of Spider-Man's most notorious enemies, Norman Osborn becomes the Green Goblin after experimenting with a serum that grants him superhuman strength and agility.
  • Doctor Octopus: Also known as Doc Ock, Otto Octavius is a brilliant scientist who gains four mechanical arms after an experiment goes wrong, making him one of Spider-Man's most dangerous foes.
  • Venom: A symbiotic alien that bonds with Eddie Brock, Venom has all of Spider-Man's powers plus a more sinister nature, often acting as an anti-hero.
  • Morbius: A scientist who transforms into a living vampire due to a failed experiment aimed at curing his rare blood disorder, resulting in superhuman strength, speed, and a thirst for blood.
